About the Author

Dr. Myra Auza is a passionate educator, devoted author, and lifelong learner with a diverse academic and professional background. From the corporate world to over 20 years in education—18 of them in the U.S.—her mission has always been to inspire and empower learners and readers of all ages.

She holds multiple degrees, including a Doctorate in Education, and has authored various educational and children’s books such as I Learn My ABC, How Al Alpaca Found Friendship Through Self-Love, and The Adventures of the Undersea Peacemakers.
